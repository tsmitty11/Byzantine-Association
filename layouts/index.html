Vision

Byzantine Association (BA) is a recognized association under Swiss law. BA engages with existing and future stakeholders to create a long lasting and ongoing program that supports the research and development of emerging technologies. Additionally, BA supports open source and transparent scientific research for emerging technologies. All research results are published under an open source license and may be freely used by anyone.
