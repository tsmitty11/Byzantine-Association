Vision

The Byzantine Association is a recognized nonprofit association under Swiss law. The BA engages with existing and future donors to create a long lasting and ongoing program of grants supporting the research and development of emerging technologies. The BA supports open source and transparent scientific research for emerging technologies. The BA provides grants to developers and scientists in Switzerland and abroad. All research results are published under an open source license and may be freely used by anyone.

BA originates grants for open source and transparent scientific research of emerging technologies.

The primary recipients are developers and scientists. Please consult the Policies page, and our standard Grant Agreement for more information. Prior to receiving a grant, an individual or business must be registered as an associate of BA. Please see the Associates page for more information. The portal is now open!
